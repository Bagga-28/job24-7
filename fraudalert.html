Fraud Alert

At JOB24/7, your safety and trust are our top priorities. We urge all users to remain vigilant and protect themselves from job-related frauds and scams.

1. Common Job Scams

  • Demanding money for job placements or interviews
  • Fake job offers asking for confidential documents
  • Jobs that sound "too good to be true"
  • Requests to download suspicious files or apps

2. How to Identify a Scam

  • Legitimate companies never ask for payment for interviews or offer letters
  • Look for suspicious email IDs not matching the company's domain
  • Avoid sharing OTPs, passwords, or sensitive documents

3. Protect Yourself

  • Apply only via trusted portals like JOB24/7
  • Research the company before attending interviews
  • Never make payments in exchange for a job offer

4. Report Suspicious Activity

If you come across a suspicious job posting, email, or recruiter, please report it immediately at: support@job247.com
